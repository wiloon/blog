web项目

默认位置：src/main/resource/log4j.xml, 编译后打成war包... xxx.war/WEB-INF/classes/log4j.xml

指定其它位置:

[xml]
  
<context-param>
  
<param-name>log4jConfigLocation</param-name>
  
<param-value>/WEB-INF/log4j.xml</param-value>
  
</context-param>
  
[/xml]
