1.如何固定表格宽度[HTML]
  
<TABLE style=&#8221;table-layout: auto | fixed&#8221;>
  
auto: 默认值
  
fixed: 宽度固定,截断超出的内容

<TABLE style=&#8221;word-break: normal | break-all | keep-all&#8221;>
  
normal: 默认值,按字词截断换行
  
break-all: 强行截断并换行
  
keep-all: 不截断不换行

style=&#8221;TABLE-LAYOUT: fixed; WORD-WRAP: break-word&#8221;
