（1）表tb1有如下数据：

 姓名                     语文                        数学                    物理

 张三                       68                           89                        99

 李四                      90                            66                         78 

现在要求写出查询语句得到如下查询结果  name                    subject                       score

 张三                       语文                           68

 张三                       数学                           89

 张三                       物理                            99

 李四                       语文                            90

李四                      数学                            66

李四                       物理                            78 

<p>
sql语句如下：

select 姓名 as name,’语文’ as subject,语文 as score from tb1

union select 姓名 as name,’数学’ as subject,数学 as score from tb1

union  select 姓名 as name,’物理’ as subject,物理 as score from tb1

 order by name 
或者：
</p>

<p>
select * from(<br /> select 姓名 as name,’语文’ as subject,语文 as score from tb1<br /> union<br /> select 姓名 as name,’数学’ as subject,数学 as score from tb1<br /> union<br /> select 姓名 as name,’物理’ as subject,物理 as score from tb1<br /> )tb<br /> order by name
</p>

<p>
（2）tb2表有如下数据：
</p>

<p>
name              subject                  score
</p>

<p>
张三               语文                       74<br /> 张三                英语                      88<br /> 张三                物理                       90<br /> 李四                语文                      88<br /> 李四                英语                      67<br /> 李四                物理                        95
</p>

<p>
 
</p>

<p>
通过查询得到如下数据：
</p>

<p>
姓名         语文                  英语                     物理
</p>

<p>
张三          74                   88                        90<br /> 李四           88                    67                        95
</p>

<p>
 
</p>

<p>
sql语句如下：
</p>

<p>
select name as ‘姓名’,<br /> max(case subject when ‘语文’ then score else 0 end) 语文,<br /> max(case subject when ‘英语’ then score else 0 end) 英语,<br /> max(case subject when ‘物理’ then score else 0 end)物理<br /> from tb2<br /> group by name
</p>

<p>
现在要求写出查询语句得到如下结果：
</p>

<p>
姓名         语文                  英语                     物理            总分                平均分
</p>

<p>
张三          74                    88                        90               252                    84<br /> 李四           88                    67                        95                250                    83.33
</p>

<p>
 
</p>

<p>
sql:
</p>

<p>
select name ‘姓名’,<br /> max(case subject when ‘语文’ then result else 0 end) 语文,<br /> max(case subject when ‘物理’ then result else 0 end) 物理,<br /> max(case subject when ‘英语’ then result else 0 end)英语,<br /> sum(result) as 总分,<br /> avg(result) as 平均分<br /> from tb<br /> group by name
</p>
