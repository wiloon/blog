### 字符串比较

<pre><code class="language-go line-numbers">fmt.Println("go"=="go")
fmt.Println("GO"=="go")

fmt.Println(strings.Compare("GO","go"))
fmt.Println(strings.Compare("go","go"))

fmt.Println(strings.EqualFold("GO","go"))
```

Compare函数，区分大小写，比自建方法“==”的速度要快
