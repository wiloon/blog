## 改主机名, hostnamectl
http://blog.csdn.net/peterxiaoq/article/details/72912862

```bash
  
hostnamectl
  
hostnamectl status
  
hostnamectl -static
  
hostnamectl -transient
  
hostnamectl -pretty
  
sudo hostnamectl set-hostname new-host-name

```
